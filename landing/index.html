All Your Trip Needs in One Place

From the first idea to the last sunset, Navigator keeps your crew organized, connected, and on budget.

Collaborative Itineraries

Build your trip plan together in real time. See the latest schedule, vote on activities, and stay in the loop. No more scattered Docs and Sheets.

Smart Expense Splitting

Track expenses easily. Connect your expenses to trip participants and activities while Navigator does the rest. Navigator calculates who owes what. No more awkward payment requests, just settle up in Navigator.

Built-in Group Chat

Keep all trip conversations in one place. Share links, photos, and polls without losing them in a sea of family and work messages.

Share a single invite link and your whole crew joins instantly. No app downloads required, no sign-up hoops — just tap and you're on the trip.
